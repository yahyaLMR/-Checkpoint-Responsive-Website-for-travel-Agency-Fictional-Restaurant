OUR MENU

Spaghetti Carbonara

A rich and creamy Roman pasta dish made with spaghetti, eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, guanciale (cured pork cheek), and black pepper. The heat from the cooked pasta gently cooks the eggs, creating a silky sauce without cream. The crispy guanciale adds a salty, savory depth, making it a beloved comfort food.

Risotto alla Milanese

A luxurious saffron-infused risotto originating from Milan. Made with Arborio or Carnaroli rice, butter, onions, white wine, beef or vegetable broth, and Parmigiano Reggiano, its vibrant yellow color comes from saffron threads. The dish is creamy yet al dente, often served alongside ossobuco (braised veal shanks).

100MAD

Margherita Pizza

The iconic Neapolitan pizza that embodies the colors of the Italian flag: red (tomato sauce), white (mozzarella), and green (fresh basil). The simple yet perfect combination is spread on a thin, chewy crust baked in a wood-fired oven. Named after Queen Margherita of Savoy, it remains a symbol of Italian culinary tradition.
